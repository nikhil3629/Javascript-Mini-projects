  • Sai Charan

    Hi, I'm Sai Charan, a passionate photographer based in Anantapur. I specialize in portrait, wedding and lifestyle photography, capturing authentic moments that tell a story.

    I've been exploring the art of photography for over five years, starting with street photography during my travels.

    My work focuses on highlighting natural light and raw emotions to create compelling visuals.My images have been featured in Modern Lens magazine.

    Photography, to me, is about freezing time and creating memories, and I look forward to bringing my vision to new projects
